What subjects and courses does EduAI support? +
EduAI currently supports major subjects including Computer Science, Mathematics, Chemistry, History, Psychology, English, Physics, and Biology. We're continuously expanding our database to include more departments and specialized courses. The question bank is organized by department, semester, and subject for easy navigation.
Is EduAI free to use? +
Yes, EduAI is currently completely free for all college students. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will remain accessible to students. We believe in making quality educational assistance available to everyone.
Do I need to create an account? +
You need to sign in with your Google account to use EduAI. This allows us to save your chat history, personalize recommendations, and provide a better user experience. We use Google OAuth for secure authentication and don't store your Google password.

ü§ñ AI Search Help

How do I ask effective questions? +
Be specific and clear: Instead of "Tell me about loops", ask "Explain for loops in Python with examples".

Provide context: Mention your subject, topic, or unit when relevant.

Use academic language: Terms like "define", "explain", "compare", "analyze" work well.

Ask follow-up questions: Build on previous answers for deeper understanding.
What types of questions can I ask? +
You can ask about definitions, explanations, examples, comparisons, problem-solving steps, formulas, historical facts, theoretical concepts, and practical applications. EduAI works best with academic questions related to college-level subjects.
Why do some answers have different confidence levels? +
Confidence levels indicate how certain our AI is about the answer based on available information. High confidence (80%+) means the answer is well-supported by our knowledge base. Medium confidence (50-79%) suggests the answer is likely correct but may need verification. Low confidence (<50%) indicates you should double-check the information.
Can I access my previous questions? +
Yes! Your chat history is automatically saved and displayed in the "Recent Searches" section. You can click on any previous question to view the full conversation or re-run the search. Use the refresh button to update your history.

üìö Question Bank Guide

How do I navigate the Question Bank? +
Step 1: Select "Question Bank" mode from the main page
Step 2: Choose your Department from the dropdown
Step 3: Select your Semester
Step 4: Pick your Subject
Step 5: Browse units and click to expand question categories
Step 6: Click on individual questions to view answers
What are the different mark categories? +
Questions are organized by mark weightage:

2-Mark Questions: Short answer questions requiring brief explanations, definitions, or simple calculations.

15-Mark Questions: Long answer questions requiring detailed explanations, derivations, problem-solving, or comprehensive analysis.
Can I copy questions and answers? +
Yes! When you select a question, you'll see copy buttons that allow you to copy the question only or copy both the question and answer together. This makes it easy to create your own study notes or share with classmates.

üîß Troubleshooting

üí° Quick Tip

Most issues can be resolved by refreshing the page or signing out and signing back in. If problems persist, try the solutions below.

Sign-in issues +
Can't see the Google sign-in button: Disable ad blockers or privacy extensions temporarily

Sign-in fails: Clear browser cookies and cache, then try again

Account not recognized: Make sure you're using a Google account, not other email providers

Popup blocked: Allow popups for ai.aasaitech.in in your browser settings
Search not working +
No results or errors: Check your internet connection and try refreshing

Slow responses: Our servers might be busy; wait a moment and try again

Incorrect answers: Try rephrasing your question or being more specific

Search button not responding: Press Enter in the search box as an alternative
Question Bank not loading +
Dropdown menus empty: Refresh the page and try selecting options in order

Units not expanding: Click directly on the "+" button or unit title

Questions not available: Some course combinations may not have questions yet

Content not displaying: Disable browser extensions that might interfere with JavaScript
Mobile device issues +
Layout problems: Rotate your device or zoom out if content appears cramped

Buttons too small: We've optimized for touch; try tapping precisely on button centers

Slow performance: Close other browser tabs to free up memory

Keyboard issues: Use the mobile keyboard's "Done" or "Go" button to submit searches
Browser compatibility +
EduAI works best on:
Recommended: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+

Features requiring JavaScript: Make sure JavaScript is enabled

Older browsers: Some features may not work; please update your browser

Private/Incognito mode: May affect Google sign-in; try regular browsing mode
