Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection

Last Updated: September 18, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Name and email address from your Google account
  • Profile picture (if available)
  • Google user ID for account identification

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use EduAI:

  • Search queries and questions asked
  • AI responses and interaction history
  • Course selections (department, semester, subject)
  • Usage statistics (search count, session duration)
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and general location data

1.3 Academic Content

  • Questions and answers from your interactions
  • Course material preferences and selections
  • Study patterns and learning analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Provide AI-powered syllabus assistance and answers
  • Maintain your chat history and search records
  • Personalize content based on your academic preferences
  • Enable seamless sign-in and account management

2.2 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI algorithms
  • Enhance user experience and interface design
  • Develop new features and educational content
  • Monitor system performance and reliability

2.3 Communication

  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Provide educational tips and feature announcements

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored securely on cloud servers located in India, with backup systems ensuring data reliability and availability.

3.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure authentication using Google OAuth 2.0
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular automated backups with encryption

3.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active. Chat history and search records are kept to improve your experience unless you request deletion.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For secure authentication
  • AWS/Cloud Services: For hosting and data storage
  • Analytics Services: For usage statistics (anonymized data only)

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

4.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of the business assets, with continued privacy protection.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

  • View and download your personal data and chat history
  • Update your profile information through your account settings
  • Delete specific search queries or entire chat history
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format

5.2 Account Management

  • Sign out from all devices and revoke access tokens
  • Deactivate your account temporarily
  • Request permanent account deletion

5.3 Privacy Settings

  • Control data collection preferences
  • Manage notification settings
  • Opt out of analytics data collection

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication, session management, and core functionality.

6.2 Analytics Cookies

Optional cookies help us understand user behavior and improve our service. You can opt out through your browser settings.

6.3 Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your preferences and maintain session state.

7. Children's Privacy

EduAI is designed for college students (18+ years). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

While our primary servers are in India, some third-party services may process data internationally. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and maintain appropriate safeguards.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or prominent notice on our platform. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
