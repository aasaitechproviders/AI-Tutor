Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection

Last Updated: September 18, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Aasai Tech Providers Private Limited, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our AI-powered educational platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you sign in with Google to access our AI educational platform, we collect:

  • Name and email address from your Google account
  • Profile picture (if available)
  • Google user ID for secure account identification

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our AI platform:

  • Search queries and questions asked to our AI system
  • AI responses and interaction history
  • Course selections (department, semester, subject)
  • Usage statistics (search count, session duration)
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and general location data
  • Performance metrics and system logs

1.3 Academic Content

  • Educational questions and AI-generated answers
  • Course material preferences and selections
  • Study patterns and learning analytics
  • Question bank interactions and bookmarks

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Provide AI-powered educational assistance and intelligent answers
  • Maintain your chat history and personalized search records
  • Customize content based on your academic preferences and needs
  • Enable seamless sign-in and secure account management
  • Deliver relevant educational content and recommendations

2.2 AI System Improvement

  • Train and enhance our AI algorithms for better educational support
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve response accuracy
  • Develop new AI features and educational capabilities
  • Monitor system performance and optimize response times
  • Ensure quality control and accuracy of AI responses

2.3 Communication and Support

  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and technical support requests
  • Provide educational tips and new feature announcements
  • Deliver customer support and troubleshooting assistance

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage Location and Infrastructure

Your data is stored securely on enterprise-grade cloud servers primarily located in India, with redundant backup systems ensuring data reliability and 99.9% availability. We utilize industry-leading cloud infrastructure providers with robust security measures.

3.2 Comprehensive Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption (TLS 1.3) for all data transmissions
  • Secure authentication using Google OAuth 2.0 protocol
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
  • Automated encrypted backups with geographic redundancy
  • Network security monitoring and intrusion detection
  • Data anonymization and pseudonymization techniques

3.3 Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide services. Chat history and search records are maintained to enhance your learning experience and improve our AI system, unless you specifically request deletion. Inactive accounts may be archived after 24 months of non-use.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Service Integrations

We integrate with the following carefully vetted third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In Services: For secure user authentication and identity verification
  • Cloud Infrastructure Providers: For hosting, data storage, and system reliability
  • Analytics Services: For usage statistics using anonymized and aggregated data only
  • AI/ML Service Providers: For enhancing our educational AI capabilities

4.2 Legal and Compliance Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by applicable Indian laws, court orders, or regulatory requirements, or to protect our legal rights, user safety, and platform integrity.

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets involving Aasai Tech Providers Private Limited, your information may be transferred as part of the business assets, with continued privacy protection under this policy or a substantially similar policy.

5. Your Rights and Privacy Controls

5.1 Data Access and Portability

  • View, download, and export your personal data and complete chat history
  • Access detailed records of your AI interactions and learning patterns
  • Request machine-readable formats of your data for portability
  • Receive regular data summaries and privacy reports

5.2 Account and Data Management

  • Update your profile information and preferences at any time
  • Delete specific search queries, conversations, or entire chat history
  • Sign out from all devices and revoke access tokens
  • Temporarily deactivate or permanently delete your account
  • Request data correction or rectification of inaccurate information

5.3 Privacy and Communication Settings

  • Control data collection preferences and analytics participation
  • Manage email notification and communication settings
  • Opt out of optional data processing activities
  • Set data retention preferences for your content

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

6.1 Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies that are strictly necessary for authentication, session management, security, and core platform functionality. These cannot be disabled without affecting service operation.

6.2 Analytics and Performance Cookies

Optional cookies help us understand user behavior, measure performance, and improve our AI educational services. You can control these through your browser settings or our privacy preferences.

6.3 Local Storage and Session Management

We use browser local storage and session storage to save your preferences, maintain login state, and provide a seamless user experience across sessions.

7. Children's Privacy Protection

Our AI educational platform is designed primarily for college students (18+ years). We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age. If we become aware of such collection, we will take immediate steps to delete the information and terminate the account.

For users under 18 who may access educational content with parental consent, we implement additional privacy safeguards and require parental verification.

8. International Data Processing

While our primary data processing occurs within India, some third-party services may process data internationally. We ensure all international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws, including implementing appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and adequacy decisions.

9. AI and Machine Learning Data Processing

9.1 AI Training and Improvement

We use aggregated and anonymized interaction data to train and improve our AI models, ensuring better educational support while maintaining your privacy.

9.2 Automated Decision Making

Our AI system makes automated recommendations for educational content and study materials. You have the right to request human review of any AI-generated recommendations that significantly affect your learning experience.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data security breach that poses risks to your privacy, we will notify affected users within 72 hours and provide detailed information about the incident, its impact, and remedial actions taken. We will also notify relevant regulatory authorities as required by law.

11. Policy Updates and Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or privacy practices. Significant changes will be communicated via email, in-app notifications, or prominent notices on our platform at least 30 days before taking effect.

Your continued use of our AI educational platform after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms. You may choose to discontinue using our services if you do not agree with the changes.
